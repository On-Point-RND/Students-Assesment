Introduction

Focus on colored MNIST dataset, specifically:
Source Domain: Digit "3" with various colors
Target Domain: Digit "2" with various colors
Goal: Transform images of digit "3" into digit "2" while preserving the original color style
Explored two main methods:
Reproduction of the DDIB (Diffusion-based Image Bridges) framework
Implementation of DDPM latent space transformations to guide the image translation process
