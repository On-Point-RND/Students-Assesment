Problem statement

Aim: to develop a novel methodology for the assessment of full-size rock subjected to fracture, based on machine vision techniques.

Objectives: using machine learning and computer vision techniques to develop approaches for automatic:
- fracture detection (counting)
- fracture angle determination (inclination)
- fracture opening width estimation
